Case Study

Rescue Me Pools — Custom Dashboard + AI Outreach

Turning 550 old leads into booked jobs

A custom-coded dashboard and automated outreach system that helped a Gilbert, AZ pool service company reactivate their entire dead lead list — and start booking jobs within days.

1

The Problem

Rescue Me Pools had over 550 old contacts sitting untouched in their system. No follow-up, no pipeline visibility, no way to tell which leads were still interested. They were paying for marketing but letting past leads go to waste.

2

The Solution

I built a custom-coded dashboard with an AI-powered outreach system that automatically contacted all 550+ leads, staged them through different pipelines based on their responses, and gave the owner full control — including the ability to pause automation and jump in with manual texting when a lead was ready to close.

3

The Result

Within the first week, the system had booked over 10 jobs from leads that had been collecting dust. The owner could see every metric, every pipeline stage, and every conversation in one place — and knew exactly where to focus to close more work.
